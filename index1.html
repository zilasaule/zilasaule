We are Ilya Donets and Ri Tori (Margarita Shchetinskaia) artists with the main focus on storytelling, graphic design and illustration. We took Zilasaule (which means the blue sun in Latvian) as our common name in oder to designate our belonging to the visionary vise-versa-world. // We make books investigating (and trying to invent) new forms and media which assumed to replace books. // Also we run Samoglaz interactive magazine project. It is intended as a field for artistic comprehension of being for different creatives from all over the world.
